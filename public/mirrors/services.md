# Evolve Therapy Services - Our Services

## Holistic Therapy Management
At Evolve we provide all of the services that you require to provide your patients with the highest possible level of care and service.

### Service Categories
1. **Optimal Therapy Outcomes**
   - Clinical Analysis for PDPM case mix efficiency-opportunity.
   - Business Intelligence customized to your market.
2. **Medicaid Case Mix Analysis & Quality Measures**
   - Education and Analysis of your site’s QMs.
   - Education of your SNF staff, marketing and administration.
3. **Progressive Programs**
   - Optimizing therapy service reimbursement (MPPR optimization).
4. **Therapy Cost Reduction**
   - Optimizing staffing models for operational success.
   - Appropriate therapy delivery and clinical discharge planning.

### The Evolve Difference
- Your staff grows internally and remains YOUR team.
- Customized CEU/education for therapists and LTC staff.
- Seamless transition from third-party therapy companies to in-house models.

### Transition Models
- **Presently Third-Party?**: We transition your team to in-house while you retain the revenue.
- **Already In-House?**: We provide recruitment, denial management, education, and data analysis expert resources.
